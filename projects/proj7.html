¡BENDITO CAOS!

2024

IDENTITY

¡BENDITO CAOS! is a celebration of the cultural revolution that was La Movida in 1980’s Spain.

Born as a musical special edition, this project thrives on exploring every cultural aspect of the movement. A commemorative bundle including a publication, a makeup kit and condoms, is accompanied by a website.

With a visual identity featuring striking patterns and bold colors, this project invites you to embrace the rebellious spirit of the Movida Madrileña and celebrate its enduring legacy.

Created with Pedro Matos and Bernard Gerber, at FBAUL.
