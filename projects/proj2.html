EDITORIAL

Publication exploring the conflicting feelings that arise when embarking on the new challenge that is an experience abroad, and its role in finding one's character and sense of self.

The translucent paper invites the reader to want to know more, almost as if to understand the whole narrative at once. However, only by carefully examining each page can the reader understand the full message: much like only through living each moment without worrying about the future can one enjoy life abroad.
