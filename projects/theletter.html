THE LETTER

2023

EDITORIAL

The Letter is a publication spotlighting the lives and legacies of design and typography maestros. Each edition is complemented by pull-outs for comparisons with other designers.

In this issue, the goal was to grasp the essence of Herb Lubalin — both his corporate and artistic facets are given the spotlight. This issue features lifelong colleagues and friends Lou Dorfsman and George Lois, highlighting the intricate relationship among the three, and the ways in which they influenced each other.

Created with Pedro Matos and Bernard Gerber, at FBAUL.
