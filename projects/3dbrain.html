3D BRAIN

2024

EDITORIAL

"3D BRAIN" is a publication-diary that explores the frontier between the objects that accompany us and their version in our imagination.

Defining 3D scans as a medium, I could then explore the concept of objects that accompany life — specifically, the objects that accompanied me during my time in Barcelona. From there, their digital manipulation paved the way to materializing the experiments in a catalog, which ultimately became a kind of personal diary. Each object told a vivid story, creating networks of meaning with other items.

Created at BAU Barcelona.
